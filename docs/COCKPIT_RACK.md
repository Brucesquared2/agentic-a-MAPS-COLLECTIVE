## How to curate Claude's "Treasure Trove" of Calls
1. Collect saved Claude invocation logs from `Sunshine_Digital/key_log.yml` or archived files.
2. Curate the most narratable entries (the "key calls").
3. Insert highlights under Claude Capsule → Key Calls in this manifest.
4. Keep the full invocation history in the ledger for auditability; surface highlights in this manifest for quick reference.

This manifest + ledger pairing gives you a both a narratable summary and a complete audit trail for capsules and companions in the cockpit.
